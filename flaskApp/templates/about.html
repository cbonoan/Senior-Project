Our Team

The team consists of a group of four experienced software developers attending California State University of Bakersfield as Seniors. We are all very passionate about helping out those who are going through tough times in isolation, and are aiming to help people get their life back on track. All four developers have experience with designing database systems, expert systems, and front end development. We hope to promote happiness and success in our users lives.
