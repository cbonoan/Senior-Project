About Us

Our Mission

The goal of MindSanctuary is to help you become more accustomed with how you are currently feeling. It can be a difficult topic for most of us to discuss when it comes to our emotions and showing vulnerability in the process of recognizing your feelings can be overbearing and sometimes uncomfortable. Our interactive quiz is meant to assist with this problem. Implementing a custom designed algorithm, our program discovers the best sources of Books, Music & Articles to provide you with material for your current state-of-being.

Our Team

The team consists of a group of four experienced software developers attending California State University of Bakersfield as Seniors. We are all very passionate about helping out those who are going through tough times in isolation, and are aiming to help people get their life back on track. All four developers have experience with designing database systems, expert systems, and front end development. We hope to promote happiness and success in our users lives.
