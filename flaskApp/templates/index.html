Our Purpose

One's current state-of-being is incremental to the longevity of a flourshing mental health. To live in the present is to understand one's emotional state and prepare contentedness for the future self. Be truthful and reflect deeply to help bring awareness to the places where attention is needed. This application will assist with fine-tuning one's emotional state and is meant to coincide with our innate ability to look perspectively inward. Our features can help precisely identify the type of emotion one is feeling and give way to further reflection. Recommendations are presented upon the arrival of a final concise emotion that will further strengthen a healthy mentality. Your mental health is one the most important aspects of your well-being, give it the care it deserves. Welcome to the Sanctuary for your Mind.
